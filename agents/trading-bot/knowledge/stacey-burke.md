# Stacey Burke Trading Methodology

Session-based, price action trading focused on Asian Consolidation Breakouts (ACB).

## Core Principle

Each session has a purpose. Trade the breakout from Asian consolidation during London/NY sessions using pure price action.

---

## ACB (Asian Consolidation Breakout)

### The Pattern

1. **Asian Session**: Market consolidates, forms range
2. **London Open**: Breakout from Asian range
3. **Trade**: Breakout direction with trend

### Identification

1. Mark Asian session high and low (00:00 - 06:00 UTC)
2. Wait for London open (07:00 UTC)
3. Watch for decisive breakout
4. Confirm with price action

### Entry Rules

- Enter on first pullback after breakout
- Stop loss inside the Asian range
- Target 2-3x the Asian range size

---

## Three-Day Market Cycle

### Day 1: Setup Day
- Range formation
- Identify key levels
- NO TRADES

### Day 2: Breakout Day
- Breakout from Day 1 range
- PRIMARY TRADING DAY
- Enter on confirmation

### Day 3: Extension Day
- Trend continuation or reversal
- Watch for exhaustion
- Take profits or manage position

---

## Session Purpose Framework

### Monday
- **Purpose**: Weekly range formation
- **Action**: Mark highs and lows
- **Trading**: Avoid or small size only

### Tuesday
- **Purpose**: First directional move
- **Action**: Watch for fakeouts
- **Trading**: Enter on confirmed breakout

### Wednesday
- **Purpose**: Primary trend day
- **Action**: Full commitment
- **Trading**: MAIN TRADING DAY

### Thursday
- **Purpose**: Continuation or reversal
- **Action**: Protect profits
- **Trading**: Trail stops, reduce size

### Friday
- **Purpose**: Profit-taking
- **Action**: Close positions
- **Trading**: AVOID new entries

---

## Price Action Focus

### What to Watch

1. **Structure**: Higher highs/lows or lower highs/lows
2. **Key Levels**: Support/resistance, round numbers
3. **Candle Patterns**: Engulfing, pin bars, inside bars
4. **Volume**: Through price action (large candles)

### What NOT to Use

- Lagging indicators (moving averages for entries)
- Oscillators (RSI, MACD for primary signals)
- Complex setups

## Gold Trading (XAUUSD)

Stacey Burke's specialty - 3 trades per day on gold.

### Characteristics

- High volatility
- Clear session moves
- Respects ACB patterns

### Strategy

1. Mark Asian range
2. Enter London breakout
3. Manage through NY session
4. Close before end of day

---

## Opening Range Framework

### Concept

First 30-60 minutes establishes the day's battleground.

### Trading

1. Mark opening range high and low
2. Wait for breakout
3. Trade breakout direction
4. Target 2x opening range

### Initial Balance

First hour of trading = Initial Balance (IB)
- Trade breakout from IB
- IB extension targets: 1.5x, 2x, 2.5x IB range
