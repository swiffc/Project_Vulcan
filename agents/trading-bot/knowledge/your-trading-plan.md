## Trade Criteria Checklist

### Before Entry
- [ ] Is it Tuesday-Thursday? (Avoid Monday/Friday)
- [ ] Are we in London or NY session?
- [ ] Is bias aligned with True Open?
- [ ] Has liquidity been swept?
- [ ] Is there a clear FVG or OB for entry?
- [ ] Is R:R at least 1:2?

### Entry Trigger
- [ ] Price returns to OTE (61.8-78.6% fib)
- [ ] OR price enters FVG
- [ ] OR price touches Order Block
- [ ] Confirmation candle closes in direction

## Position Sizing Formula

```
Position Size = (Account Balance × Risk %) / (Entry - Stop Loss in pips × Pip Value)
```

Example:
- Account: $10,000
- Risk: 1% = $100
- Stop: 20 pips
- Pip value: $10/pip (1 lot)
- Size: $100 / (20 × $10) = 0.5 lots

## Entry Rules

### Primary Setup: ACB + ICT Confluence

1. Asian range marked
2. London opens with breakout
3. Wait for pullback to:
   - FVG, or
   - Order Block, or
   - OTE zone
4. Enter on confirmation candle
5. Stop loss beyond structure

### Secondary Setup: Liquidity Sweep Reversal

1. Identify equal highs/lows
2. Wait for sweep of liquidity
3. Watch for CHoCH or MSS
4. Enter on retracement to FVG/OB
5. Stop beyond sweep point

---

## Exit Rules

### Take Profit
- TP1: 1:1 R:R (move stop to breakeven)
- TP2: 1:2 R:R (take 50% off)
- TP3: Next liquidity target (let rest run)

### Stop Loss
- Initial: Beyond entry structure
- After TP1: Move to breakeven
- Trail: Behind swing points

### Time-Based Exit
- Close before major news
- Close before London close (if in profit)
- Close before end of NY session

## Emergency Protocols

### If Down 3% Daily
- STOP trading for the day
- Review what went wrong
- Return tomorrow fresh

### If Down 6% Weekly
- STOP trading for the week
- Deep review of all trades
- Identify pattern in losses
- Return next week with adjustments

### If On Tilt
- Step away from screens
- 15-minute break minimum
- Return only when calm
